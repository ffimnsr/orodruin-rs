# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- critique code and ideas, not people
- assume good intent while being direct about technical concerns
- give actionable review feedback
- keep discussions focused on correctness, safety, and maintainability

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or threats
- repeated bad-faith disruption of review or discussion

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, lock, hide, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation for repeat or severe violations.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact maintainers privately when possible. Public escalation is acceptable when no private route is available or immediate moderator action is required.
